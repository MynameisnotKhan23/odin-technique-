Doro Wat Recipe

Description

Discover Doro Wat,a tantalizing ethiopian chicken dish infused with a medley of spices and berere seasoning

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of unsalted butter,divided
  • 1 onion,chopped
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 can tomato paste
  • Three-quarters cup berbere seasoning
  • 1 teaspoon chopped garlic
  • Half a teaspoon ground ginger
  • 4 skinless,boneless chicken breast,cubed
  • One third of a cup sweet white wine
  • Half a teaspoon ground cardamom
  • Half a teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 4 hard-cooked eggs

Directions

  1. Melt a half cup of butter in a skillet over medium-low heat.Add onion:cook and stir until translucent, 5 to 6 minutes.Add a half cup of water and tomato paste;Stir until hot,about 2 minutes. Stir in berbere,remaining a half cup butter,garlic and ginger. Reduce heat to low;cook until mixture thickens to a paste consistency,20 to 30 minutes.

  2. Stir remaining 2 cups water into berbere paste;add chicken. Simmer until thickened to sauce consistency,about 45 minutes.

  3. Stir wine,cardamom,and black pepper into sauce;add hard-cooked eggs. Cook until sauce is slightly reduced,about 15 minutes more.
