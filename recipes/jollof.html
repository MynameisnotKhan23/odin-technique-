Jollof Rice Recipe

Description

A very popular west African dish that will test your spice buds!

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 large onion,sliced
  • 1 can of stewed tomatoes
  • 1 can tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon black pepper
  • ¼ teaspoon red peppers
  • ½ teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon chopped fresh rosemary
  • 2 cups of water
  • 1 whole chicken,cut into 8 pieces
  • 1 cup of uncooked white rice
  • 1 cup of diced carrots
  • ½ pound fresh green beans,trimmed and snapped into 1 to 2 inch pieces
  • ¼ teaspoon ground nutmeg

    • Steps

    1. Pour oil into large saucepan. Cook onion in oil over medium-hot low heat until translucent
    2. Stir in stewed tomatoes and tomato paste, and season with salt, black pepper, cayenne pepper, red flakes, Worcestershire sauce and rosemary. Cover, and bring to a noil. Reduce heat, stir in water, and aff chicken pieces. Simmer for 30 minutes
    3. Stir in rice,carrots, and green beans, and season with nutmeg. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low. Cover, and simmer until the chicken is fork-tender and the rice is cooked,25 to 30 minutes
